METAGLASS

Metaverse sunglasses with passive income.

A DeFi NFT sunglasses for the metaverse.

Earn PASSIVE INCOME as you hold.

Each Metaglasses holds 1000 shares, which is used to calculate profits. Profit is calculated by adding a holders total shares, then sending all funds to their wallet.

External investment

Part of the cost is invested into a hedge fund earning the holders profit.

Shared Royalties

Every-time a Metaglasses is sold royalties will be split among holders.

Generative Art Filters​​​​

Each NFT mints a unique algorithmically generated video synth seed acting as the lens. This seed can be later used to recreate effects on any platform. 

Video synth can also be synced with music creating unique effects.

Frequently Asked Questions

More Questions
How do I get paid?

Profits are sent directly to glasses owners wallet.

How is the profit split?

Each Metaglasses holds 1,000 shares which is used to calculate how much each glasses will earn.

What happens if I own multiple Metaglasses??

The total amount of share is calculated and one larger payout is made. For example if you own 3 metaglasses the total shares you hold is 3,000 and will be paid accordingly. 

Where can I use my Metaglasses?

Metaglasses will be accessible in the metaverse, and eventually our own Entertainland.
